Who We Are

A Business Built
on Principles

YourNearBest Plumbing Services was founded in 2009 by master plumber James Harlow, who started the business with a single truck and a simple belief: do honest work, charge fair prices, and treat every customer like a neighbor.

Over 15 years, that philosophy has turned a one-man operation into a team of 12 certified plumbers serving hundreds of homes and businesses across the region. We've grown because our customers trust us — and we take that trust seriously every single day.
